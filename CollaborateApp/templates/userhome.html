Groups:
Groups correspond to teams that you can create or someone else can add you. You can add users to the group by their username. You can search them under the user search if you are unaware of their username.The tabs correspond to various groups that you belong to. You can access a group by clicking on it.
Files:
Click on the group name to open the files that belong to that group. Click on a file to open it in the editor. Files in a group are restricted to the users of the group ad cannot be accessed by someone not belonging to the group. To ensure security, check before you add a user to the group.
Personal Files:
All your personal files are stored in {{ request.user }}_Personal and can be accessed only by you. To share with your group, you can upload the file into your group and the file can be deleted from your personal files (not suggested for important files as group files can be edited, deleted and accessed by any user of the group).
